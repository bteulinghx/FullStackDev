Profile 7: Digital Marketing Agency
Business Overview

"Peak Digital" is a full-service marketing agency helping small businesses grow their online presence through SEO, social media management, and content creation. They prioritize data-driven strategies and transparent reporting.

Target Audience

Small business owners, startups, and local service providers who lack an in-house marketing team.

Unique Selling Points

No long-term contracts (month-to-month service), a dedicated account manager for every client, and a dashboard for real-time ROI tracking.

Business Objectives

To generate more inbound leads through the website and showcase their success through detailed case studies.

Design Requests

A modern, professional, and sleek corporate website. It should use infographics to explain services and feature a portfolio of past success stories.

Possible Sections

Sections for the website could include Home, Services, Case Studies, Team, Blog, Free Audit, and Contact Us.

Dynamic Data Ideas

Recent client wins/stats, latest marketing news ticker, and a blog feed of industry tips.

Text & Images

There are no texts and images ready-to-use. The client asks you to make up texts or generate them using a text generation tool like ChatGPT. For images, the client wants you to use copyright-free stock photos. AI-generated images are also allowed.
